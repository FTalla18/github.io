About "Drive Luxuriously"

Our Journey

Founded in April 2020, Drive Luxuriously began with a vision to turn a simple car rental idea into a thriving business. During the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, we discovered an opportunity to make our idle vehicles earn while helping others find reliable transportation.

The Founding Story

Our story began when we listed our first car, a "Red Kia Optima 2012," on Turo while navigating the uncertainties of being furloughed. Despite initial challenges, including a canceled booking due to a lack of an alternative ride, we proved the concept. In December 2020, we expanded our fleet with a "Silver 2017 Tesla Model S 75," becoming the first to offer an electric vehicle in our area. This venture, along with our initial economy car, paved the way for a successful and growing rental service.
